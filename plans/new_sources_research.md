# Research Findings: New Public Data Sources

## 1. Ashdod Port Statistics
*   **Source:** Ashdod Port Company & Israel Shipping and Ports Authority.
*   **Data Available:** Quarterly and annual financial/operational reports, including container throughput (TEUs), vehicle unloading, bulk cargo, and general cargo.
*   **Recent Trends:** 2024 saw a 1.8% YoY increase in container throughput (681,000 units) and a significant 9.6% surge in vehicle unloading due to the cessation of activity at Eilat Port.

## 2. Suez Canal Authority (SCA) Navigation Statistics
*   **Source:** SCA Navigation Statistics Dashboard and Annual Reports.
*   **Data Available:** Number of transiting vessels, net tonnage, total revenue, and ship-type breakdowns (Tankers, LNG, Bulk, etc.).
*   **Recent Trends:** 2024 saw a ~50% drop in traffic and a >23% drop in revenue due to Red Sea security challenges, with a relative recovery starting in late 2025.

## 3. Israel Central Bureau of Statistics (CBS) Trade Data
*   **Source:** Israel CBS (cbs.gov.il) under "Foreign Trade in Goods by Country".
*   **Data Available:** Monthly and annual reports on exports and imports of goods (excluding diamonds), trade deficit, and trade by region.
*   **Recent Trends:** Strong export growth to the EU (+22.3% in Sep-Nov 2025) and a decrease in imports from China (-19%).

## 4. OCHA Humanitarian Data Exchange (HDX) Middle East
*   **Source:** Greater Middle East Crisis Page, OCHA ROMENA, and oPt Data Portal on HDX.
*   **Data Available:** Common Operational Datasets (CODs), conflict incidents (ACLED), displacement tracking (IOM/UNHCR), and food security/health data.
*   **Features:** Available in CSV, XLSX, GeoJSON, and API via HDX HAPI, using HXL tags for easy integration.

## 5. UNCTAD Maritime Transport Statistics
*   **Source:** UNCTADstat Data Center and Review of Maritime Transport.
*   **Data Available:** Merchant fleet size, seaborne trade volumes, port performance, and Liner Shipping Connectivity Index (LSCI).
*   **New Features:** A new country-level seaborne trade dataset was released in April 2025 for granular monitoring of trade performance and supply chain integration.
